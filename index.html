About me

Did you ever try to break a pattern? it’s difficult but very rewarding at the same time! This is what I am challenging myself to do on everyday basis within my Code, my Muay Thai routine or by defeating a boss in my latest video game!

What is define me the most is the movement . It all started when I was a teenager and the moment I discovered that I wanted to explore the world.

In 2011 I left my country to come in Canada with the purpose of learning English and discover a new culture. After 7 years I am now a Canadian citizen !

During this 7 years the movement never left me, I jumped from a position to another and moved from an industry to another. This year I realize that I could use this movement to specialize myself in a field that constantly moving: “the tech industry”. That is how I turned to be a web developer , I knew that my capacity to be easily-adaptable to any new environment will help me to achieve this goal.
